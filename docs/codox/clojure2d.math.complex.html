clojure2d.math.complex

Complex numbers functions. Based on Apache Commons Math.

Complex number is represented as Vec2 type (from clojure2d.math.vector namespace).

To create complex number use complex or ->Vec2.

Categories

  • Power / logarithm: exp log pow
  • Trigonometry: acos asin atan cos cosh csc sec sin sinh tan tanh
  • Other functions: abs add arg complex conjugate div mult neg reciprocal sq sqrt1z sqrt sub

Constants

  • I = [0.0, 1.0]
  • I- = [0.0, -1.0]
  • ONE = [1.0, 0.0]
  • TWO = [2.0, 0.0]
  • ZERO = [0.0, 0.0]
